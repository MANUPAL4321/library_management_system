Frequently Asked Questions

How does the online book request system work?

Students can log in and request books online. Admins approve the requests, and the books are issued to the students.

What are the automated reminders?

Our system sends automated reminders to students and admins after 15 days of book issuance to ensure timely returns.

How can I renew a book?

Students can log in to their account and request a renewal for the issued book. Admins can approve the renewal online.
